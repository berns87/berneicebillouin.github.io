Meet the Team


Fig. 1- Members of staff at a retreat in Tradewinds Hotel San Fernando.

Our team at Deryck's Bakery Delights is made up of 24 passionate and energetic individuals committed to excellence. Each branch operates with a skilled team of 2 bakers, 4 frontline representatives, and 2 backroom assistants, ensuring top-quality service and delicious baked goods.

Our History


What started as a small family-run bakery, has grown into a thriving limited liability with three branches across Trinidad & Tobago.
Deryck Billouin has been the intial founder and owner of Deryck's Bakery Delights. The bakery has been in operation for the past 5 years. Its growth in the year 2023, welcomed the opening of 2 more outlets for operations. This opened doors for more staff as well as more customers both locally and abroad.

Mission Statement


At Deryck's Bakery we are committed to bringing communities together by providing delicious, high-quality, freshly baked goods. Because we take pride in what we do, we are dedicated to delivering exceptional customer service, while building strong relationships with our customers, and delivering quality, tradition, and innovation in every bite.
